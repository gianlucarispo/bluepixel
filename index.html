The best DN iOS client.

Enjoy the best way to check Designer News on your iPhone. Built specifically for iOS 7, BluePixel is an easy to understand and a delight to use app, that has a pleasant user interface to check the front page and recent DN stories. Checking out stories has never been so much fun.
Download BluePixel for iOS, Available on the App Store.

Recent. Top.

Scroll through the hottest posts on Designer News right now, or through the most recent posts. Never miss a story again. It's incredibly easy to use

In App stories

BluePixel is perfect for reading. To head to the story link, just tap on a story to open it in-app. You can also chose an external browser like Safari or Chrome.

Upvote & Comment

Just use a quick gesture to upvote and comment stories, it’s just one tap and slide. Right or left? It’s up to you.

Full read later integration

With Pocket, Instapaper and Readiblity support built right-in, it's easy to save things to your queue. Just tap and hold on a story.

Let it be dark.

Use it everywhere and everytime. Even at nightime, bedtime or when your eyes can’t stand so much whitespace.
